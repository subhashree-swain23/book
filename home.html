About Our Rooms & Resources

Meeting Rooms

Our meeting rooms are designed for small gatherings and brainstorming sessions, accommodating up to 12 people.

Conference Rooms

Ideal for larger meetings, our conference rooms are equipped with advanced audio-visual support for seamless presentations.

VIP Rooms

Reserved for high-priority meetings, our VIP rooms offer privacy and exclusive facilities for up to 5 attendees.

Seminar Rooms

With a seating capacity of 50, our seminar rooms are perfect for lectures, training sessions, and larger presentations.

Resources

We provide essential resources like projectors, whiteboards, and high-performance laptops to meet all presentation and collaboration needs.

How to Use the Booking System


Step 1

Select a room that fits your needs, whether it’s a small meeting room or a large seminar space.

Step 2

Choose your time slot. Our system shows real-time availability for all rooms and resources.

Step 3

Add any additional resources you require, like projectors or laptops.

Step 4

Confirm your booking with a simple click. An email confirmation will be sent to your inbox.
